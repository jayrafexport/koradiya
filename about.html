Who We Are

A Leading Manufacturer of Porcelain & Ceramic Tiles

Koradiya Group Impex is a leading manufacturer of Porcelain and Ceramic tiles in India. Our manufacturing units run on modern, closely-monitored equipment, and the Indian consumer's fast-moving appetite for style and finish shapes every new design we release.

Our pace in keeping up with customer and market demand has made Koradiya Group Impex synonymous with quality, service and innovation — not only in the domestic market but internationally too. Our Porcelain Tiles, Ceramic Wall Tiles, Floor Tiles and Designer Tiles come in a wide range of colours and textures, suited to bathrooms, living rooms, corridors, study rooms and kitchens alike.

  • ✓ Eco-friendly production, following environmental compliance at every stage
  • ✓ In-house design studio creating uniquely patterned tiles
  • ✓ German digital printing technology for sharp, low-variance output
  • ✓ A manufacturing & export hub connecting Asia to the global market
