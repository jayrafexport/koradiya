About Koradiya Group Impex

Trust, Quality, Transparency & Reliability

Koradiya Group Impex is a manufacturer and exporter of Porcelain Tiles, Digital Vitrified Tiles and Marble Slabs, built on one working principle: every batch that leaves our floor should be one we would put in our own homes. We work closely with traders, distributors and importers to deliver consistent colour, sharp print definition and dependable packing — shipment after shipment.

01Mission

Meet every customer's expectation on quality, colour consistency and delivery timeline — without exception, on every order size.

02Vision

To rank among the top five tile and slab manufacturers in India, driven by continuous investment in technology and design research.

03Values

Trust, respect and honesty in every interaction — with the trader placing a trial order and the importer placing a container load alike.

How It's Made

Raw Material to Ready-to-Ship, in Six Stages

Every slab and tile moves through the same controlled sequence — the reason our shade and size variation stays within tolerance across a full container.

01

Raw Material Selection

Clay, feldspar and quartz sourced and lab-tested for consistency.

02

Batching & Grinding

Precise recipe blending, wet-milled into a uniform slurry.

03

Pressing & Shaping

High-tonnage hydraulic presses form each body to spec.

04

Digital Glazing

German digital printing lays down design with minimal variance.

05

Firing

Kiln-fired at controlled temperature for strength & finish.

06

Sort, Pack & Dispatch

100% quality-checked, palletised and loaded for export.
