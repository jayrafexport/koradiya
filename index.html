Company Profile

Koradiya Group Impex is a leading manufacturer of Porcelain and Ceramic tiles in India. Our manufacturing units are equipped with cutting-edge modern technology, and the Indian consumer's rapidly growing appetite for style and aesthetics is the inspiration behind every design we create.

Our pace in keeping up with customer and market demand has made Koradiya Group Impex synonymous with quality, service and innovation — not only in the domestic market but internationally too. Our Porcelain Tiles, Ceramic Wall Tiles, Floor Tiles and Designer Tiles come in a wide range of colors and textures, complementing bathrooms, living rooms, corridors, study rooms and kitchens alike.

  • ✓ Eco-friendly products, with our manufacturing plant following environmental policies
  • ✓ Uniquely designed tiles for your dream space
  • ✓ Latest digital printing technology from Germany, for minimal color variation and maximum sharpness
  • ✓ Manufacturing & export hub connecting Asia to the global market
