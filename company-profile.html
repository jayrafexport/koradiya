About Koradiya Group Impex

The largest manufacturer of Porcelain and Ceramic tiles in India, built on trust, quality and transparency.

KORADIYA GROUP IMPEX is the largest manufacturer of Porcelain and Ceramic tiles in India, with manufacturing units equipped with cutting-edge modern technology. The Indian consumer's rapidly growing appetite for style and aesthetics is the inspiration behind every design we make — and our pace in keeping up with customer and market demands has made Koradiya Group Impex a byword for quality, service and innovation, in both the domestic and international market.

Our range spans Porcelain Tiles, Ceramic Wall Tiles, Floor Tiles and Designer Tiles, in a wide variety of colours and textures — complementing bathrooms, living rooms, corridors, study rooms and kitchens as an extension of each room's own character. With an unwavering commitment to quality, we continually adapt to new technology and standards as they evolve.

Be it technology, research, design or quality — Koradiya Group Impex focuses on all of these to adopt new production methods and improve product quality. Our team's creativity and design ability shape a product line built on both innovation and uniqueness, guided by eco-friendly manufacturing practices across our plant.
